Hi there!
I’m a Spanish–Guatemalan children’s illustrator from Barcelona, where I grew up in a multicultural home, enjoying fresh tomatoes, painting lessons, and sunny days.
I moved to Finland to pursue a Master of Arts at Aalto University, and for the past eight years, I’ve been living near Helsinki, working from my home studio — a place where I often see squirrels climbing trees, birds singing in a hundred languages, and sometimes even a mother deer with her fawn, or a fox who doesn’t seem to mind my presence.
My illustrations are inspired by nature, and I’m especially drawn to stories that celebrate diversity, curiosity, and inclusion.
Whether you’re a publisher, author, or dreamer with a story to tell, I’d love to hear from you!
